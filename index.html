About Hon. Solomon

Solomon Saigut Cherogony is a community-focused leader from Eldama Ravine (Benonin), with strong experience in economics, finance, and development planning—shaped by professional exposure in Romania, South Africa, DR Congo, and Chile.

Public Service Leadership

  • Board Chair, Eldama Ravine Sub-County Hospital
  • Strengthened governance and financial discipline
  • Improved staff morale and service delivery
Leadership that delivers, not just talks.

Education Sector Support

  • Served on multiple school boards in Baringo
  • Prioritized essentials under limited budgets
  • Built modern sanitation units at Koibatek Primary
Schools first. Children first.

Peace & Community

  • Conflict resolution and community dialogue
  • Mentorship for youth studying abroad
  • Employment creation through local ventures
Development thrives where peace exists.
